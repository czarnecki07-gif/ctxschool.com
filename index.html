What is CTXSCHOOL?

A calm, practical model for schools using the CTX System.

It supports the existing teaching process with interactive materials, CTX explanations, lessons, quizzes and educational paths.

Better learning

Text, images, video, audio and CTX explanations help students understand difficult topics.

Less teacher overload

Reusable materials, worksheets and quizzes reduce repetitive preparation work.

Nobody gets lost

Knowledge can be organised clearly so students, teachers and parents understand where they are in the learning process.

Gradual implementation

A school can start with one teacher, one subject or one collection and expand step by step.

AI transparency

AI-supported elements can be clearly marked, edited and used responsibly.

School identity

The school can build a modern educational image without losing its own character.

School potential

A school where learning is clearer and teaching is lighter.

CTXSCHOOL helps schools use the CTX System in a practical way: for students, teachers, school leadership and educational institutions.

For studentsClear explanations, interactive media and structured materials support different learning speeds.
For teachersMaterials can be reused, improved and shared instead of being prepared from scratch every time.
For schoolsThe school can promote modern learning, support its teachers and organise its educational resources.
For parentsClear materials and school communication can help explain how modern learning with CTX works.

It is a practical school program based on CTX materials, school profiles, verified teachers and gradual participation in the CTX System.
