General principles

CTXSCHOOL supports education. It does not replace the school.

The program is intended for schools, groups of schools, universities, libraries, educational institutions and organisations conducting educational activity.

Not an LMS

Not an e-register

Electronic registers remain separate. CTX supports knowledge and learning materials.

No student fees

Students do not pay for participation. Materials are made available by the teacher, school or institution.

School participation

The school can join as a CTXSCHOOL Participant™.

Gradual use

Participation can start partially, experimentally and step by step.

School autonomy

The school decides the scope of use and the number of participating teachers.

Benefits for schools

A modern educational image without unnecessary complexity.

The school can use CTX to support teachers, organise materials and promote educational achievements.

School codeThe school may use its own code and promote materials connected with the institution.

Teachers keep the rights to their materials.

The school can support and promote its authors, but it does not take over copyright ownership of their work.

Joining the program

Simple participation, introduced gradually.

The expected participation fee may be around 100 PLN per year for the institution. It does not apply to students. Details may be adjusted in the final cooperation rules.

Step 1

The school expresses interest in CTXSCHOOL Participant™.

Step 2

The scope of participation is selected: pilot, selected teachers or wider institutional use.

Step 3

The school receives identification and promotional materials.

Step 4

Teachers can create, publish and organise CTX materials.

Step 5

The school profile can present teachers, publications, courses and achievements.

Step 6

The program can grow gradually with the school.
